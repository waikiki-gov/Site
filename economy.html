Waikiki Economy

World's Wealthiest Nation | State-Guided Market Capitalism

Waikiki's economy has experienced remarkable growth since 1999, transforming from a GDP of $75 billion to $41.8 trillion by 2025. Through strategic state guidance, technological innovation, and prudent fiscal management, the nation has achieved unprecedented prosperity with near-zero inflation and full employment.
