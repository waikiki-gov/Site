Royal Stories

Raimondo and Bailey
Their Shared Story

Prince Raimondo Chease and Bailey Pickett's relationship began aboard the Seven Seas High School and deepened through their university years and Senate work. Their time together, from autumn 2011 to February 2017, tells a story of young love and shared growth, defined by mutual respect and shared ambitions.

Meeting at Seven Seas High School

Raimondo and Bailey's story began in 2011, when they were both admitted to the unique, around-the-world educational program of Seven Seas High School, operating aboard the luxury cruise ship SS Tipton. Although they came from completely different backgrounds—Raimondo, as the heir to the Waikiki royal family, lived the carefree life of billionaires, while Bailey brought her nature-loving values from Kettlecorn, a tiny American farming town—fate soon crossed their paths. Their introduction began with a minor accident: right after enrollment, by the cruise ship's juice bar, Raimondo accidentally spilled a glass of soda on his future classmate, Bailey. With his usual chivalry and generosity, Raimondo immediately offered to buy her a new outfit as compensation. During their shopping trip together, Raimondo and Bailey got to know each other much better, marking the beginning of a special connection. Bailey soon realized that behind the confident prince hid a sensitive and intelligent boy, while Raimondo was completely enchanted by the girl's resourcefulness and intelligence.

Studies Aboard the SS Tipton

The school year spent at Seven Seas High School proved to be a highly productive and eventful period for both of them, during which they formed close friendships with their extraordinary classmates. They studied together aboard the SS Tipton with Zack Martin, whose laid-back attitude and constant pranks always ensured carefree fun. They also spent a lot of time with the cruise ship owner's daughter, the somewhat naive but big-hearted billionaire heiress London Tipton, whose extravagant life of luxury often sharply contrasted with Bailey's rural simplicity, yet they became genuine friends. Former child star Marcus Little also belonged to their close circle of friends, and they shared many common experiences on board and during port excursions. Despite their differences, Raimondo and Bailey became increasingly in sync in both their studies and personal lives. Their relationship deepened month by month during the cross-ocean journey. Beyond school assignments and friendly gatherings, they became each other's support system, so much so that they arrived at the graduation prom as a confident, official couple. Their shared adventures aboard the SS Tipton soon became the center of attention, and news about the special young couple made headlines worldwide.

Shared Travels on the Cruise Ship

As students of Seven Seas High School, Raimondo and Bailey could travel the whole world aboard the SS Tipton. Their first embrace happened in New York, and it was here that Raimondo officially introduced Bailey to the press. In Rio de Janeiro, photos of them exploring an ancient jungle ruin during an adventurous excursion made international front pages. In Beijing, enthusiastic fans welcomed them before they toured the Imperial Palace, the Great Wall, and admired the historic monuments of the Chinese capital. Naturally, they also stopped by Bailey's hometown, Kettlecorn, where Raimondo met her family and experienced the traditional farming lifestyle. During their further adventures, they marveled at the stunning Pyramids of Giza and the Sphinx in Cairo, and vacationed together with Raimondo's family in the exotic volcanic islands of Hawaii. In New Delhi, at the invitation of the Indian Prime Minister, they discussed climate protection at an elite charity gala. During their round-the-world trip, they gained countless new experiences. They visited the Vatican Museums in Rome, the Opera House in Sydney, dove into the vibrant metropolitan nightlife in Tokyo, while in Quebec, Canada, a baseball game and a speedboat bay tour made the journey unforgettable.

Performance at the 2012 Nova Aurelia Olympics

One of the most memorable stops of their cruise journey was definitely Nova Aurelia, where the young couple attended the grand opening ceremony of the 2012 Olympics. The invitation was a special honor, important to them not only diplomatically but also personally. As the highlight of the event, Bailey took the stage to perform in front of an audience of tens of thousands in the packed national stadium and millions more watching on television. After weeks of rehearsals and excitement, Bailey performed her original song 'Country Girl' with stunning confidence. She wore a dazzling, silvery sparkling stage outfit that perfectly suited the grandeur of the event, while her voice and natural stage presence instantly enchanted the audience. At the end of the song, the stadium practically erupted with applause. From the royal box, Raimondo watched his girlfriend's brilliant performance with excitement and immense pride, acknowledging the success with a huge smile. This unforgettable evening meant not only Bailey's first truly serious public performance but also became one of the most beautiful shared memories of their relationship.

Completion of the Palace and Joint Furnishing

February 2014 was a milestone in the young couple's life, as the construction of Raimondo's breathtaking palace on the picturesque coast of Nova Aurelia was completed. The stunning, futuristic building complex was a generous gift from the prince's parents for his sixteenth birthday and his successful university admission. Bailey also played a prominent role in designing the monumental residence's interiors and surroundings, leaving her mark on the design. The girl's deep love for nature, refined aesthetic sense, and rural charm perfectly harmonized with Raimondo's modernist, technology-focused vision. Thanks to the palace's massive floor area, smart home systems, and unmatched luxury amenities, it remains one of the world's most unique and valuable properties to this day. For Bailey, the dearest project was designing the glass-domed tropical greenhouse built in the palace's vast garden, where she passionately cared for rare, exotic plants, and which soon became the couple's most intimate and favored shared retreat from everyday bustle. During the joint design period, Raimondo had a state-of-the-art observatory tower built in the east wing of the palace so they could stargaze together in the evenings.

Raimondo and Bailey on the Set of The Matrix

In the spring of 2014, Raimondo tried his hand in the Hollywood film industry, specifically as a producer right away, working on the fourth installment of the cult Matrix film series. Since the massive film studio behind the production was owned by his family, it was an excellent opportunity for the young prince to prove his aptitude in the world of cinema in action. Naturally, his girlfriend Bailey accompanied him to the tropical location for the months-long shooting period in Brazil. Although Bailey didn't take a role in the film, she was present alongside the crew every day, supporting her boyfriend and learning the behind-the-scenes secrets of filmmaking. While Raimondo worked with the crew, Bailey enthusiastically took advantage of the opportunities offered by Brazil's unparalleled nature. She frequently went on exploratory hikes in the surrounding untouched rainforests, where she documented and photographed rare, endangered plant and animal species. A highly successful charity exhibition was later organized from these artistically demanding nature photographs. Another defining outcome of the shoot was that the couple met Selena Gomez here, who had an important role in the film. A deep and genuine friendship developed almost instantly between Bailey and the global superstar singer, which was only further strengthened by their shared passion for protecting the planet and their commitment to charity work.

University Life and Studying Together

In September 2012, Raimondo and Bailey began their studies together in the prestigious bachelor's program in economics at Waikiki Economics University. At one of the world's highest-ranking universities, Raimondo and Bailey made a perfect team, mutually helping each other throughout the years. Bailey's outstanding analytical, mathematical, and statistical talents complemented Raimondo's comprehensive, global strategic thinking and leadership skills incredibly well. They spent plenty of time in the campus libraries working out complex end-of-semester projects together, sat next to each other in lectures with hundreds of students, and during exam periods, thanks to Bailey's regular and precise tutoring, Raimondo was also able to finish among the best. Their differences often showed in everyday university life: while Raimondo drove into the campus parking lot daily in the world's most expensive luxury sports cars, Bailey usually made the trip by bicycle, staying true to her deeply rooted, eco-conscious life philosophy. However, this apparent sharp contrast beautifully exemplified the unique dynamic of their relationship, in which the prince's extravagant generosity and the girl's grounded modesty formed a harmonious whole.

Bailey's Environmental Projects

In the fall of 2014, alongside her university studies, Bailey dedicated her time to a personal cause close to her heart. She founded the environmental initiative called Green Horizon, which aimed to protect Waikiki's rainforest ecosystems. Raimondo supported his girlfriend's project wholeheartedly, not only financially but also through his personal presence at numerous volunteer events. Bailey passionately believed that sustainability and economic development are not mutually exclusive. The initiative's first major success was a comprehensive, weeks-long forest cleanup campaign, joined by hundreds of university students and local residents, preventing tons of plastic waste from entering nature. Besides fieldwork, Bailey held numerous lectures and interactive workshops for the university community on the importance of renewable energy sources, reducing ecological footprints, and responsible consumption. Green Horizon soon outgrew being a simple student organization, and with Raimondo and Bailey's collaboration, they also tested a new solar-powered irrigation system in the university's botanical garden. During his parliamentary visits, Raimondo proudly mentioned Bailey's project as an excellent example of the social responsibility of Waikiki's youth.

Shared Vacation in Europe

In the summer of 2014, Raimondo and Bailey took a six-week European tour, which became one of the most beautiful shared experiences of their lives. The trip began in Paris, where the couple took romantic walks along the banks of the Seine, viewed the Eiffel Tower at sunset, and toured the halls of the Louvre on a private guided tour. In London, they walked past Buckingham Palace and watched a West End musical. In Barcelona, they admired Gaudí's architectural masterpieces, then spent a few carefree days on the beach at the Costa Brava. In Florence, the paintings of the Uffizi Gallery captivated them both, especially Botticelli's 'Primavera', which became Bailey's favorite painting. In Rome, Raimondo organized a special dinner at a restaurant near the Pantheon, where the couple was granted exclusive night-time access to the ancient monument. In Venice, they rode gondolas on the canals, and in Vienna, they attended an opera performance. Concluding the trip, they relaxed for a few days on the island of Santorini, where the contrast of white and blue houses and the breathtaking sunsets were forever etched into their memories. These weeks were not just about carefree relaxation, but also about enjoying their youth away from public pressure. Moreover, the rich history and culture of the European continent served as deep inspiration for both of them in their later work.

Raimondo and Bailey Become Senators

May 18, 2015, brought a historic turning point in Waikiki's political life when Chease Young, with the broad support of parliament, established the institution of the Senate. Among the appointed founding members was the young couple with impressive achievements, Raimondo and Bailey. At the solemn senatorial swearing-in ceremony, they appeared elegantly, holding hands, flawlessly reflecting the historical weight of the moment. From the very beginning, the young senators took a firm direction, perfectly complementing each other's work. While Bailey considered it her mission to resolutely represent environmental, educational, and social issues, Raimondo aimed at economic innovation, technological advancement, and strengthening international diplomatic relations. In their very first weeks, they jointly proposed an epoch-making, comprehensive national sustainability and education legislative package, which the body received with unanimous enthusiasm. During parliamentary debates, both argued with stunning confidence, eloquently, and incredibly well-prepared, constantly supporting each other's speeches. The press soon began referring to them as the Senate's most dynamic political duo, as their presence guaranteed that economic modernization and green perspectives would henceforth prevail in harmony in every government decision.

In Kettlecorn, Bailey's Homeland

In the summer of 2015, the couple visited Kettlecorn, Bailey's hometown, a tiny, traditional farming community in the Midwestern United States. The visit provided Raimondo with special insight into Bailey's roots and the world that shaped his girlfriend's nature-oriented values. The billionaire heir immersed himself in rural life with surprising humility and openness. He repaired fences with Bailey's father, Clyde, and together with her mother, Mary Jane, they prepared a traditional southern dinner. The small farming community soon took the friendly prince to their hearts, who, at the end of the visit, promised support for the renovation of the local school. While Bailey showed him her favorite childhood spots, Raimondo admitted in a later interview that it was here, close to nature, that he truly understood what made his girlfriend so special. In the autumn of the same year, Bailey's little brother, Jacob, was born, which further deepened the relationship between the Pickett family and Raimondo. The prince welcomed the newborn with a cradle, and later, during frequent visits home, he also took the boy into his heart. The arrival of the little brother opened up new perspectives in Bailey's life and also shaped her political focus, as she turned increasingly dedicatedly toward family and child protection legislation. The press and fans alike loved those intimate, human moments when later the young senatorial couple, joined by Jacob, walked in the parks of Nova Aurelia.

Joint Appearances and Award Ceremonies

Raimondo and Bailey regularly appeared together at the most prestigious international award ceremonies and cultural events. At the 2015 Billboard Music Awards, Bailey appeared by Raimondo's side in a stunning white Marchesa gown, immediately landing her on the best-dressed celebrity lists. Bailey's natural beauty and refined style perfectly harmonized with Raimondo's elegant appearance, and the press frequently referred to the pair as Waikiki's most photogenic couple. Later that year, Raimondo and his team also attended the CES technology conference in Las Vegas, where Raimondo gave a presentation on technological innovation, and Bailey participated as an invited expert in a panel discussion on sustainable technologies. At the 2016 Young Hollywood Awards, Bailey won the award for the most eco-friendly star, which she accepted with a radiant smile while Raimondo applauded from the audience. During the evening, Raimondo received the title of the year's most trending star, enriching the couple with two awards in a single night. These joint public appearances not only further increased the global popularity of the young politicians but also provided them with a perfect platform to draw the world's attention to important social and green issues by leveraging red-carpet glamour.

2016 Government State of the Year Address

At the government's grand start-of-year assessment meeting on January 7, 2016, Chease Young appreciatively praised the outstanding achievements of the newly established Senate's first year. One of the event's most touching moments was when Bailey received a custom-designed artificial blue diamond as a special recognition. The stunning jewel was crafted exclusively for her by the master jewelers of the prestigious Waikiki National Jewelers to symbolize her unwavering commitment to protecting the planet. The diamond's radiant deep blue shade evoked the color of the oceans, which Bailey so passionately loved and defended. During the formal meeting, she gave a detailed account, backed by data and an impressive presentation, of the first-year, expectation-exceeding successes of the Green Horizon environmental program, and her speech was rewarded with a standing ovation from the senators present. After the meeting, Chease Young personally congratulated Bailey in front of the cameras, emphasizing that the young senator's tireless work is exemplary proof on an international level of how public service and nature conservation can be harmoniously reconciled. At the international press conference concluding the event, Raimondo and Bailey stepped up to the microphones together to announce the revolutionary details of the complete digitization and phasing out of cash transactions planned for 2018, particularly highlighting the move's significance in curbing the shadow economy.

Miss Teen Universe Awards and Beauty Pageant

At the specially significant 2016 Miss Teen Universe Awards, Bailey achieved another outstanding success. In the career of the merely twenty-year-old artist, this was already her third beauty pageant victory, complemented by a first-place finish in a prestigious talent show. Bailey's enchanting naturalness, intelligent answers, and confident stage presence equally impressed the jury and the audience. Raimondo cheered for his girlfriend from the front row of the auditorium, and at the moment of victory, he was among the first to congratulate her with a bouquet of flowers and a warm embrace. The competition's runner-up was her close friend, Selena Gomez, who welcomed Bailey's victory with great sportsmanship. After the award ceremony, the three friends, Raimondo, Bailey, and Selena, celebrated together at an exclusive restaurant, where they enjoyed a carefree evening away from the press cameras. In her interview after the victory, Bailey emphasized that beauty is not merely an external trait, but also a reflection of inner values and a sense of responsibility for the environment.

The Amazon Expedition and the Visit to the Kennedy Space Center

In the summer of 2016, Raimondo and Bailey set off on a special adventure tour to the South American continent. The days spent in the Amazon rainforest were of particularly great significance for Bailey, as a childhood dream came true when she could see the world's densest tropical jungle with her own eyes. Accompanied by an experienced naturalist, the couple discovered rare plant species, and Bailey documented several new orchid varieties, which were later added to the collection of the Nova Aurelia Museum of Natural History. After the Amazon adventure, Raimondo and Bailey visited the Kennedy Space Center, where they gained comprehensive insight into the history and present of American space exploration. As the first stop of the visit, they viewed the lunar module model, then the tour touched upon the launch pads, and at the end of the program, they got a glimpse into a laboratory where astronauts are prepared. Bailey was particularly interested in the environmental applications of Earth observation satellites and asked the researchers numerous questions about monitoring climate change from space.

Trip to England

The London stay of Waikiki's diplomatic delegation turned out to be extremely eventful, one of the highlights of which was that Raimondo, Bailey, and the other young senators received an invitation to Great Britain's most popular morning television show, 'This Morning'. During the studio conversation, the politicians not only provided British viewers with a behind-the-scenes look at modern international public life but also shared their personal experiences in England. Even during the live broadcast, Bailey maintained her magical, natural aura and honest, open personality, with which she immediately stole the hearts of the viewers and the hosts. When she spoke passionately about the international successes of the Green Horizon program and Waikiki's rock-solid commitment to environmental protection during the interview, the studio audience expressed its appreciation with a spontaneous, loud standing ovation. Beyond the official programs, the couple made time to explore the British capital. They made a lengthy visit to the British Museum, where Bailey studied the priceless ancient Egyptian exhibition in fascination. Following this, they took a romantic, long walk in picturesque Hyde Park, during which Bailey, who adored botany, introduced the prince to the fine, orderly principles of classical English landscaping culture and Victorian garden design with great enthusiasm and expertise.

Opening of the Advanced Programming University

September 1, 2016, was a huge celebration for Waikiki's education, when, within the framework of the national school year opening, Raimondo and Bailey honored the opening of Waikiki's very first purely corporate-funded higher education institution, the Apple-funded Advanced Programming University, with their personal participation. As the highlight of the event, at the official ribbon-cutting, Bailey stood close beside Raimondo, then stepped to the microphone and, in an inspiring speech, drew attention to the fact that cutting-edge technological education and profound environmental awareness are now inseparable concepts. Within the framework of the event, the young senator proudly unveiled an innovative digital platform that uses advanced sensors and real-time data to monitor the current state of Waikiki's fragile ecosystems. The student organization developed this pioneering software in close collaboration with Apple's lead engineers, making it open-source for the students of the newly opened university, providing an incredible opportunity for practical research. In his own celebratory speech, Raimondo firmly assured the first-year students that through this unique private university program, on perhaps the most modernly equipped campus in the world, they would master the knowledge of the future from professors considered the most excellent internationally.

Master's Program and Diplomatic Travels

From September 2015, Raimondo, Bailey, and Selena continued their studies together in the master's program at Waikiki Economics University, where during the four-semester postgraduate program, students spent two days a week on parliamentary work and another two days on academic activities. During the master's program, Bailey gained increasingly deep expertise in sustainable economics and environmental policy, which she combined with practical experience from her parliamentary work. Raimondo continued to show a special interest in macroeconomics and geopolitics, while Bailey excelled in the fields of environmental economics and resource management. Chease Young also personally took part in teaching with his courses 'Foundations of Modern Economy' and 'Environmentally Conscious Economy', while Jessica Walker gave lectures on the banking system. During 2016, Raimondo and Bailey participated in numerous diplomatic trips together, during which Bailey increasingly proved her diplomatic skills earned in her own right. At the grand opening of Disneyland in Rio, Bailey enthusiastically welcomed the amusement park's eco-friendly solutions and recycling systems. During a diplomatic visit to Africa, they jointly presented a detailed plan for job creation and the modernization of the education system, which was received with great interest by local leaders. During their visit to Luxembourg and Paris, the pair held several key meetings with members of the EU Commission.

Bailey and Selena's Friendship

Bailey and Selena Gomez's friendship continuously deepened from their acquaintance formed on the set of The Matrix in 2014 all the way through their senatorial years. The relationship between the two young women went far beyond a mere collegial dynamic; a true, deep friendship was woven between them, grounded in shared values, cooperation, and mutual respect. Bailey and Selena regularly met outside of Senate sessions as well, lunched together, jointly attended charity events, and occasionally shopped together in the boutiques of Nova Aurelia. Selena frequently consulted Bailey about her work as a UNICEF ambassador, and Bailey helped her friend integrate an environmental perspective into her efforts. Their bond was especially tight during international travels, where they served as each other's support and confidantes. Shared photos of Raimondo, Bailey, and Selena regularly circulated on social media, and the friendship between the three senators was highly popular among fans. Beneath the surface, however, a subtle rivalry gradually formed between the two girls over Raimondo. Bailey represented the original, deeply rooted bond, while Selena built consciously and with diplomatic elegance. Bailey instinctively sensed that her friend was drawing ever closer to Raimondo, and although the two girls never openly confronted each other, this quiet competition shaped them both. Their silent rivalry never turned bitter or friendship-destroying; on the contrary, this nuanced dynamic made their triangle simultaneously complex and humanly fascinating.

Relationship Dynamics

From 2015, when Selena became an everyday presence in Raimondo's life as a fellow senator, the connection between them slowly grew more nuanced. While Raimondo was officially in a relationship with Bailey, an increasingly intimate friendship was weaving between him and Selena. During 2015 and 2016, Raimondo and Selena spent more and more time alone together, traveling jointly, and the bond between them gradually exceeded the boundaries of ordinary friendship. Bailey, who instinctively sensed the signs of this process, did not confront anyone openly, as she deeply trusted Raimondo, yet due to their differing visions for the future, the distance between them slowly, imperceptibly began to grow. Thanks to this background, Raimondo and Selena's relationship ultimately rested on entirely different, far more complex foundations. While Bailey represented naturalness and simplicity, Selena was a more determined type who took on a far more independent and proactive role in their private life. Selena's resolute presence and devoted care gradually opened up ever more space between them, and step by step reshaped the balance.

Raimondo and Bailey's Private Life in 2016

Despite an exhaustingly packed political and diplomatic schedule full of travels and official appearances, Raimondo and Bailey took extraordinary care to nurture their private life and shared hobbies, retreating from the noise of the world. They preferred to spend weekends at the Princely Palace on their yacht or at the vast royal stud farm sprawling near the palace, where they took long, liberating horse rides on the beach. Over time, the highly dynamic sport of polo also grew into a shared passion, and the couple became regular participants in the cheerful but competitive friendly matches organized in the company of Selena and other fellow senators and friends. Furthermore, Bailey taught Raimondo the fine basics of gardening with endless patience; in the palace's lush botanical garden, nurturing special, sensitive tropical orchids together became their daily, soothing ritual. In return, the prince initiated Bailey into the mysteries of geopolitics, discussing their own future, ambitions, and most secret shared dreams.

Closing of Raimondo and Bailey's Relationship

In early 2017, both Raimondo and Bailey felt that their relationship had undergone a natural transformation. The nearly six-year-long relationship that had begun in 2011—growing out of a friendship formed aboard the Seven Seas High School—slowly entered a new phase. Raimondo himself felt that the deep bond with Selena had grown ever stronger over the past two years, and Bailey could not ignore this either. In early March, after Raimondo and Selena returned from a shared vacation in Thailand, Raimondo and Bailey, following long, honest conversations, decided to peacefully close their relationship by mutual agreement. Both considered it important to preserve their friendship, which was grounded in the deep sense of belonging built over the years they had shared together. The breakup was not dramatic or angry; it was much more the responsible and thoughtful decision of two mature young people, which both concluded by wishing the other the very best. A few days after the breakup, Raimondo and Selena officially announced that they were a couple.

Bailey Remains a Senator

Following the breakup, Bailey Pickett continued her senatorial work with unwavering determination and remained a defining figure in Waikiki's political life. Initially, the press followed with special attention how Raimondo and Bailey handled the new situation at Senate sessions, but it soon became clear that the excellent professionalism and maturity of the two former partners allowed for the seamless continuation of their cooperation. Bailey remained a champion of environmental protection, animal welfare, and education, and the gradual implementation of the Green Horizon program continued under her supervision. Selena, who had become Raimondo's new partner in the meantime, approached Bailey with respect and friendship, and the friendship formed between the two women suffered no damage. Bailey later participated in the European diplomatic tour as a member of the senatorial delegation, and walked together with Raimondo and Selena in the Louvre in Paris, making it clear to the international press that the cohesion of the Waikiki Senate was stronger than any personal changes.

Bailey's Legacy in Raimondo's Life

Raimondo and Bailey's six-year relationship left a lasting mark on both of their lives and personalities. It was Bailey who taught Raimondo the respect for nature, the beauty of simplicity, and the importance of humility. To the young prince, who grew up in the immense wealth of his parents, Bailey showed with sensitivity and wisdom that true wealth lies not in material goods, but in human connections and the responsibility felt for the Earth. Raimondo's later environmental policies, including the Blue Belt Agreement, were inspired by Bailey's influence. In a 2024 Senate debate program, Bailey advocated for stricter climate laws and achieving a carbon-neutral economy by 2030, continuing the work she started with the Green Horizon program during her university years. Bailey Pickett's story in Waikiki is not merely the chronicle of a romance, but an inspiring journey that proves: the girl who came from rural America, who believed in the power of nature and the beauty of public service, was able to shape both an empire and the mindset of a prince alike.
