National Leadership

Waikiki's leadership combines dynastic succession with democratic governance, creating a unique political system where the royal family guides the nation while elected representatives shape government policy. Leaders emphasize economic development, environmental initiatives and institutional reform that have guided Waikiki's development.

The Royal Family

The Chease family stands at the center of Waikiki's remarkable transformation. What began with Chease Young's declaration of independence in 1999 evolved into a dynasty that combines political authority with unprecedented economic success and cultural influence. Originating in the Starlight Hotels business, the family's activities expanded into finance, industry and public life, and its members have become prominent figures in both business and government.

Chease Young combined strong executive leadership with the creation of new institutions that shaped Waikiki's political model. His long partnership with Jessica Walker influenced economic policy and banking reform that prevented financial crises and revolutionized monetary transactions. Jessica's work helped modernize the Waikiki National Bank and implement regulations designed to increase system stability. Their policies and international engagement were recognized with the 2009 Nobel Prize in Economics that extended their concepts beyond Waikiki, influencing economic thinking globally.

A generational transition took place in 2017 when Raimondo Chease succeeded his father in the position of the Dictator. Born in 1996 and educated at Waikiki Economics University, Raimondo prepared for leadership through public roles, including a senate seat beginning in 2015. The transition and Chease Young becoming chancellor, showed that power transfer could occur smoothly within the dynastic framework. Raimondo emphasized technology, infrastructure and international engagement. His partner, Selena Gomez, who besides her political career, holds a public profile as an artist and humanitarian, has broadened the government's outreach to younger and international audiences.

The broader royal family plays vital roles across government and society. Angelina Chease focuses on environmental policy and joined the Monetary Council in 2024. She champions sustainable development and green finance. Jennifer Chease works on digital government and public relations initiatives. Their partners, Taylor Lautner and Tyler Posey are active in public projects and social programs. While the family is influential, each member maintains distinct roles across public, private and civic spheres with commitment to Waikiki's continued prosperity.

Meet the Royal Family

Chease Young & Jessica Walker

Chease Young founded Waikiki in 1999 and led the country until 2017. Together with Jessica Walker, they established the foundation of the democratic dictatorship, implemented major economic reforms and helped build institutions for banking and monetary policy. They were awarded the Nobel Prize in Economics in 2009 for their contributions to economic stabilization and policy design.

Founding Leader

Chease Young was born in 1972 in New York and earned an economics degree from Stanford in 1991. In 1999 he became dictator and led a political transition that resulted in the foundation of Waikiki and then pursued a program of economic and institutional reforms. Crowned king in 2005, he later stepped into a chancellor role while remaining active in international initiatives.

Economic Architect

Jessica Walker and Chease Young formed a long-standing partnership that influenced Waikiki's economic direction. As president of the Waikiki National Bank from 2008, Jessica helped shape banking regulation and financial policy, and played a central role in the country's later transition to a cashless payment system. She also served as Consul between 2017 and 2023.

Nobel Recognition

In 2009, Chease Young and Jessica Walker jointly received the Nobel Memorial Prize in Economic Sciences in Stockholm for their crisis management program during the global financial meltdown. They successfully stabilized Waikiki's economy while other nations suffered severe downturns, demonstrating innovative models for sustainable financial growth.

Global Leadership

Chease Young founded the Atomic Powers Alliance during the Pacific War in 2003, successfully preventing nuclear escalation. He established the World Government in 2018 and served as its first president. His diplomatic initiatives include the American Union formation and the United African Nations creation, along with strategic partnerships with major powers.

Royal Family

Chease and Jessica have three children who each play vital roles in national governance. Raimondo, born 1996, serves as the current dictator and crown prince. Angelina, born 1993, is a senator focused on environmental policy and serves as a Monetary Council member. Jennifer, the youngest daughter, is a senator specializing in media and communications.

Raimondo & Selena

Raimondo Chease became Waikiki's head of state in 2017 succeeding his father in a smooth transition of power. Born in 1996, he represents a younger generation of leaders and has prioritized technological initiatives and international partnerships. His partner, Selena Gomez, an artist and UNICEF ambassador who became consul and princess in 2017, serves in public roles and increased the administration's public visibility.

Modern Leader

Raimondo Chease was born on July 7, 1996 and completed graduate studies at Waikiki Economics University. He took office in 2017 and has overseen policies focused on infrastructure, international engagement and workplace reforms. His leadership has included a mix of domestic initiatives and diplomatic efforts, including four-day workweeks and joining NATO in 2025.

Artistic Leadership

Selena Gomez has taken on public responsibilities in Waikiki including a senate role and consul duties. As UNICEF's youngest ambassador, she combines humanitarian work with her political duties. She met Raimondo in 2014 during Matrix 4 filming and officially partnered with him in 2017. That same year, she was crowned Princess of Waikiki, establishing her royal status.

Space Exploration

In February 2018, Raimondo and Selena embarked on a historic two-week space mission to Waikiki National Space Station. Following extensive training, they participated in scientific experiments, conducted spacewalks and experienced zero gravity firsthand. Their journey demonstrated Waikiki's space program achievements and inspired global interest in civilian space travel.

International Diplomacy

Raimondo's diplomatic initiatives include historic Korean unification talks, visiting Russia regarding the Syria situation and partnering Israel for the Iron Dome 2 project. He met Donald Trump at Mar-a-Lago and the White House multiple times. His close relationship with Trump strengthened cooperation between the United States and Waikiki on technology, defense and trade.

Mega Projects

Raimondo announced the Mega Buildings program in 2016, featuring the Mega Pyramid, Royal Clock Tower and Miapolis vertical city. He initiated the Florida-Waikiki underwater tunnel project connecting the islands via maglev rail and highways. Under his leadership, construction began on the world's first public Hyperloop line and the Chameleon artificial islands.

Angelina & Taylor

Angelina Chease, born in 1993, serves as Waikiki's senator with a focus on environmental policy and was appointed to the Monetary Council in 2024. As the partner of Taylor Lautner, she prioritizes sustainable development, green technology and climate initiatives that intersect with infrastructure planning and define Waikiki's approach to growth.

Environmental Leadership

Angelina graduated from Waikiki Economics University and has since focused on environmental and innovation policy. Appointed senator in 2015, she has supported initiatives to promote renewable energy, green technology and circular economy approaches, and works with public and private partners with Tesla and Hyperloop on sustainable mobility projects.

Financial Expertise

Angelina joined the Chease Investment Group board in 2019, managing strategic investments across Fortune 500 companies. She owns 14 major corporations including Berkshire Hathaway, McKesson Healthcare and Verizon. Forbes ranked her second most influential businesswoman globally in 2018. In 2024, Raimondo appointed her to the Waikiki National Bank's Monetary Council.

Ocean Conservation

In 2017, Angelina and Taylor launched a campaign to reduce plastic ocean pollution, organizing beach cleanup initiatives. At COP26 Glasgow 2024, they presented the Blue Belt Agreement, the world's first multilateral coastal ecosystem protection initiative. New Zealand, Iceland and Seychelles immediately supported the agreement, with Canada and Norway expressing interest.

Youth Sports Initiative

Taylor Lautner, former Hollywood actor, joined the senate in 2015 focusing on sports and public safety. He led security reforms, community policing and youth safety programs. His youth sports motivation initiative encourages physical activity and community building. Taylor supports public transit expansion and sustainable transportation infrastructure.

Amazon Expedition

In 2024, Angelina and Taylor led a multi-week environmental expedition to Amazonia province, assessing rainforest conditions and developing sustainable forestry strategies. Working with WWF and Greenpeace, the team mapped rare species habitats. Their findings shaped senate environmental legislation and indigenous community partnerships.

Jennifer & Tyler

Jennifer Chease and her partner Tyler Posey form a dynamic senate duo focused on digital government and media innovation. Their initiatives on e-government, inclusive decision making and education modernization shape Waikiki's technological future while ensuring universal access to services for all citizens and improving public-sector efficiency.

Digital Government

Jennifer studied public finance and administration and was appointed senator in 2015. She supports budget transparency, R&D incentives and the expansion of digital public services. Her work includes efforts to improve accessibility and user experience across government platforms, and programs to help small businesses adopt digital tools.

Healthcare Innovation

Jennifer helped establish a Health Innovation Fund to support preventive technologies, telemedicine and research. She has worked on regulatory frameworks and financing models aimed at expanding access and encouraging quality-driven approaches. Together with Tyler she advocates for broader healthcare access supported by new technologies.

Technology Policy

Tyler Posey joined the senate in 2015. He supports system-level, scalable solutions that increase productivity while reducing inequality and environmental impact. His pragmatic, data-driven approach complements Jennifer's public finance focus. Together they balance rapid innovation with stable implementation through technology and institution sensitivity.

Green Infrastructure

Their Green Infrastructure Program advances smart grids, energy storage and transport electrification. The initiative strengthens domestic supply chains to increase strategic self-sufficiency. Jennifer ensures fiscal discipline while Tyler focuses on technical feasibility. Together they support the Waikiki National Bank's price stability mandate and the 2009 capital rules.

Recognition

Jennifer and Tyler received the Public Service Innovation Award for digital state service expansion and the Education Merit Medal for learning opportunity enhancement and teacher career support. Their reforms deliver tangible benefits like faster services, improved health outcomes and competitive business environment.
