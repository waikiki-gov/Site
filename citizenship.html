Waikiki Citizenship Program

Civilizational Sanctuary

The "Citizenship by Merit" program is not only reserved for the wealthy. We are welcoming inventors, doctors, entrepreneurs and artists from all around the world. Talent and diligence are rewarded. Assess your eligibility under the new system.

Available Tiers

After filling the application form, the system classifies applicants into six initial tiers based on their score. Since the high number of applicants this is a necessary step to prioritize processing. This category also gives an overview of the applicant's current standing, however does not determine the final decision. Once citizenship is granted, all citizens enjoy the same rights and benefits.

0-34 points

Red Tier

Very low probability of granting citizenship. Applicants are advised to improve qualifications before reapplying.

35-49 points

Blue Tier

Low probability of granting citizenship. Entry level with stringent screening, will probably offer permanant residency before full citizenship.

50-64 points

Bronze Tier

Medium probability of granting citizenship. For professionals and families. Residency permit available with a security deposit.

65-79 points

Silver Tier

Good probability of granting citizenship with an offer. For high net worth investors. Expedited processing for government bond and property investments.

80-90 points

Gold Tier

Very good probability of granting citizenship. For the global elite. Fast-track citizenship.

90+ points

Diamond Tier

Very good score, almost garanteed chance of granting citizenship. Reserved for state level partners.

Naturalization Process

The process consists of threee separate stages designed to ensure that applicants meet all financial, legal and social requirements for citizenship. The three steps combine automated reviews, questionaires and a personal or online interview with an immigration officer. The combined score of these steps will determine the decision on the citizenship application.

Objective Meritocracy

No bias. Only mathematically demonstrable added value is considered. Questions about Wealth, salary, professional achievements, and community involvement are evaluated.

Predictive Analysis

The 'The Gatekeeper' AI analyzes digital and financial footprints, accesses answers from various databases, and predicts the likelihood of successful citizenship application.

Cultural Cohesion

The system filters ideologies and behaviors that threaten social cohesion through a personal interview with an immigration officer.

Citizenship & Naturalization Process FAQ

What is the "Citizenship by Merit" program?

Waikiki operates a unique meritocratic naturalization system. Unlike traditional residency programs, citizenship is not merely bought but earned through a combination of economic contribution, professional expertise, and cultural compatibility. The program evaluates applicants via the Waikiki Merit Assessment Protocol (W-MAP), scoring them on factors such as net worth, strategic skills (e.g., AI, Fusion Energy), and ideological alignment with our society.

What are the Citizenship Tiers?

Based on the W-MAP score, successful applicants are categorized into four tiers:

  • Gold Tier (Strategic Asset):Immediate citizenship, VIP healthcare, and diplomatic privileges. Requires 90+ points.
  • Silver Tier (Investor):Citizenship after 1 year. Requires significant bond purchase and property investment.
  • Bronze Tier (Resident):5-year path to citizenship. Ideal for professionals and families.
  • Blue Tier (Talent):A temporary status for young digital nomads and innovators with a 10-year integration path.
What is "The Watcher" vetting process?

Security is paramount in Waikiki. "The Watcher" is our state-of-the-art AI-driven vetting system that analyzes 15 years of an applicant's digital footprint, financial history, and social connections. It ensures that new citizens are not only financially stable but also culturally coherent and pose no risk to national security or social harmony. The process is strictly confidential and objective.

How much does citizenship cost?

Costs vary dynamically based on your W-MAP score. Higher merit scores result in lower financial requirements. Generally, applicants must purchase a Loyalty Bondranging from $500,000 to $2,000,000 WUD, which is held for 5 years. Direct application fees and mandatory real estate investments may also apply depending on the assigned Tier. Gold Tier applicants enjoy significantly reduced financial barriers due to their high strategic value.

Does my family get citizenship too?

Yes, Waikiki is strongly family-centric. The nuclear family (spouse and children) of a primary applicant is included in the application. In fact, being married with children grants bonus points in the W-MAP assessment, as we view stable families as the bedrock of society. Family members also undergo health and security screenings appropriate for their age.

Is dual citizenship allowed?

Waikiki generally allows dual citizenship, recognizing the global nature of our elite citizens. However, in the event of a diplomatic or trade conflict between Waikiki and your country of origin, your primary loyalty must lie with Waikiki. Certain high-security government positions may require renouncing other allegiances.
